Excited to share my latest project: meine_dict — a Flutter app designed for students who want to learn German vocabulary in a simple, practical, and engaging way.

The app includes more than 11,000 vocabulary entries and offers different ways to learn and review words. I built it to make vocabulary learning more accessible and motivating for anyone who wants to improve their German.

This project is still growing, and improvements are very welcome. If you are interested in language learning, education, Flutter, or open-source development, I would love your feedback, suggestions, and contributions.
